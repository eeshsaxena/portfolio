This is where I collect the pieces I write for newspapers and magazines. Each
post has the original clipping (the PDF above) plus a short writeup: what the
piece was about, why I wrote it, and anything that didn't make the final cut.

## How a new post gets added

Every post is one file. To publish a new one I add a markdown file and drop in
the scanned clipping:

1. Create `app/routes/blog.<slug>.mdx` with a title, date, a one-line abstract,
   and the path to the PDF.
2. Upload the clipping to `public/blog/<slug>.pdf`.
3. Push to GitHub, and the site rebuilds itself.

That's the whole flow. Replace this welcome note with your first real article.
